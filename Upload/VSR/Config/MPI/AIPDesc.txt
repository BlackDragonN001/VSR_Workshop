AIPs are the method Combat Commander uses to set priorities for the CPU team in a IA/MPI map. They control what vehicles are built, what to attack, etc.

-=VSR AIP INFO=-

Level 1: Easy mode AIP by Boxhead
--
Level 2: More challenging AIP by Boxhead (enemies send more units)
--
Level 3: Very challenging AIP by Boxhead (enemies send a LOT of units)
--
Level 4: Enemies send strong, early hits and fight back relentlessly (by Aegeis)
--
***If at any time the enemy stops building, check their base to make sure their constructors are not "stuck." If they are, kill them to let the enemy continue to build - this is a known issue that happens on occasion! Sorry!***
